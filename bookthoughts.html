The Last Lecture Jeffrey Zaslow and Randy Pausch 10/30/2024
This book is about a man who has cancer. This man wanted to leave something for his wife and kids after his death. He was asked to do a lecture at his graduate school, and decided to take up the mantle. I really do enjoy how this author talked about his life experiences up to the lecture. It isn't just the lecture. He provides his world-view and upbringing, key moments from his life, lessons learned, and general advice to be a functional human in this world. I also enjoyed how this book had short chapters, which made me finish it quicker than usual. I decided to read this on my kindle with .epub. I read this book before and after bed which I also enjoyed. I look forward to reading more on my kindle.
